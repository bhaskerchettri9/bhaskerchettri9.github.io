My name is Bhasker Chettri. I was born on 27 September 2006 in Gangtok, Sikkim.

My father's name is Om Kumar Chettri and my mother's name is Priya Chettri.

I believe that life is unfair only for those who don't try even once. This philosophy guides the way I think, work, and make decisions.

I see life as a long-term journey where discipline, patience, and consistent effort matter more than shortcuts. I believe in building things step by step, learning from mistakes, and always moving forward no matter the situation.

My thinking is focused on growth, value creation, and building something meaningful that can last for generations.
